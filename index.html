SPIDER-MAN 4

THE BUZZARD'S REVENGE

Synopsis

Years after stepping away from the mask, Peter Parker lives a quiet life with MJ and their children. But when former Oscorp engineer Adrian Toomes uncovers hidden truths behind the abandoned spider project, his obsession turns to vengeance.

With his family at risk, Peter must decide whether to hold onto peace… or become Spider-Man one last time.
