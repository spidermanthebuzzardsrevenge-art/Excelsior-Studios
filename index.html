A FATHER'S FIGHT

Peter Parker is 38. The suit is a little loose in the shoulders, and the weather predicts the ache in his joints. He sits at the head of the table now, gray at the temples, a father to Benjy (7) and May (5). He has finally found warmth.

But the silence of his retirement is broken when his Spider-sense screams—not a ring, but a headache.

Adrian Toomes, a brilliant but disgraced geneticist who wrote the original code for the spider program, has returned. He doesn't see a hero swinging through Queens. He sees a stolen specimen. And he's coming to "recall the product."

The Synopsis

TIME TO COME HOME

"Years after retiring the mask, Peter Parker lives a quiet life with MJ and their kids. But when former Oscorp engineer Adrian Toomes uncovers the truth behind the spider project, his obsession twists him into the Vulture — a vengeance-driven force targeting Peter’s past, present, and family."

With his world collapsing, Peter must decide whether to remain the man he’s become… or embrace Spider-Man once more.

Scene: The Parker Dining Room

Scene: The Parker Dining Room

PETER PARKER (38) sits at the head of the table. It’s warm, chaotic, and lived-in. BENJY (7) flicks a pea. The pea arcs — the kind of tiny, domestic miracle that used to be his whole life.

A tiny THWIP and Peter snags it midair without looking.

MJ

Peter. We talked about this. Not at the table.

PETER

(innocent, slightly awkward) I didn’t do it. It was... a reflex. Honestly. Total accident.

Scene: NYC Rooftop — The Ambush

WHAM! Spider-Man is yanked from the roof, dragged across gravel, and hurled into the open sky as Vulture makes his presence known.

SPIDER-MAN

(struggling, half-joking) Hey—! Whoa, hold on! Sir! You’re gonna tear your hydraulics flying this fast!

VULTURE

(cold, amplified) Stop moving.
