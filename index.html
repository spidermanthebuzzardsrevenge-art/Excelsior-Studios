A FATHER'S FIGHT

Peter Parker is 38, living quietly under the weight of a life rebuilt. Retirement has given him what he thought he'd lost—stability, family dinners, and the small joys of anonymity. But peace is brittle: a new kind of pain—sharp, electric, and strangely familiar—scratches at the edge of his Spider-sense and refuses to be ignored.

Adrian Toomes, the Vulture, returns not as a petty thief but as a man consumed by a warped idea of justice: to "recall the product" — tech and inventions he believes were stolen from the working class. His methods have grown colder, his reach wider, and his targets more dangerous. When Toomes' vendetta collides with Peter's family life, the fallout is both personal and catastrophic.

Forced back into the suit, Peter must choose between the quiet safety of the life he's built and the responsibility that made him. The Buzzard's revenge threatens more than the city; it threatens the family that finally made his life worth saving.

The Synopsis

TIME TO COME HOME

"Years after retiring, a familiar threat forces Peter Parker back into a life he thought he'd left behind—only this time, the enemy wants to take everything he loves."

After years of ghosts and quiet living, Peter's attempt to protect his family collides with a Vulture who has become a crusader for vengeance. As civic unrest and corporate secrets surface, Peter uncovers a conspiracy that ties Toomes' rage to someone from his past — and to a choice Peter himself once made.

Spider-Man must outfly his past and outsmart a man who knows how to use the city against him. The result is not simply a battle of fists, but a reckoning of identity, responsibility, and the cost of protecting the people you love.
